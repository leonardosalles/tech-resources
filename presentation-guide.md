### Presentation Guide

##### To do a good presentation and go deeper in the content you need to explain:
- Why
- What
- How
- Live Coding
- Trade-offs
- Alternatives
- Wrap-up

##### You can use create slides to explain the content.
- Use bullet points
- Don't add too much text
- Use images and icons to illustrate
- You can add a funny thing if you think its cool

For a Lightning Talk you will 5 minutes, 6 to 10 slides are good.

Also, its very important to do dry-run, to find and fix any possible issues and get prepared when questions are asked.
For a presentation, you should never read the entire content, it will be boring and squared, try to understand content and explain it in a fluid way.
